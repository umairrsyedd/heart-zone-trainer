Privacy Policy

Heart Zone Trainer

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Heart Zone Trainer ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Key Point: All your data stays on your device. We do not operate any servers or collect any data remotely.

Information We Collect

Heart Rate Data

Aspect Details
What Real-time heart rate from your Bluetooth heart rate monitor
How Via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) connection
Purpose Display heart rate and calculate training zones
Storage Processed in real-time only. NOT stored permanently.

User Preferences

  • Resting heart rate and maximum heart rate
  • Zone configurations and alert preferences
  • Last connected device information

These are stored locally on your device only.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal identification (name, email, phone)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Usage analytics or tracking
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers
  • ❌ Any data transmitted to external servers

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers. Your data never leaves your phone.

Bluetooth Permissions

The app requires Bluetooth permissions to:

  • Scan for nearby heart rate monitors
  • Connect to your Bluetooth heart rate device
  • Receive real-time heart rate data

These permissions are used solely for core functionality.

Location Permission (Android 11 and below)

On Android 11 and below, location permission is required by the Android operating system to scan for Bluetooth devices. This is a system requirement, not our choice.

  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT store location data
  • Android 12+ users are NOT asked for location permission

Compatible Devices

Heart Zone Trainer works with any Bluetooth Low Energy heart rate monitor:

  • Polar (H10, H9, OH1, Verity Sense)
  • Garmin (HRM-Pro, HRM-Dual)
  • Wahoo (TICKR, TICKR X)
  • Whoop (4.0 and newer)
  • Coospo, Magene, and other BLE HR monitors

Third-Party Services

Heart Zone Trainer does not integrate with any third-party services:

  • ❌ No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ❌ No advertising
  • ❌ No crash reporting services
  • ❌ No social media integrations

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Uninstall the app - All data is automatically deleted
  • Clear app data - Go to Android Settings → Apps → Heart Zone Trainer → Storage → Clear Data

Your Rights

  • ✅ Access - View your settings anytime in the app
  • ✅ Modify - Change your settings anytime
  • ✅ Delete - Uninstall app or clear data
  • ✅ Portability - N/A (no personal data collected)
